  2. Locate the downloaded file in your Downloads folder.
  3. Double-click the installer icon.
  4. Windows might show a blue prompt titled "Windows protected your PC." Click "More info" and then select "Run anyway."
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to finish the installation.

🛠️ Solving Common Errors

You may encounter a few common blocks during the setup process. Most errors occur due to security settings or missing system components.

Installation Failed
If the installer quits without finishing, check if you have administrative permissions on your account. Right-click the installer and choose "Run as administrator." This gives the program permission to create the necessary folders on your hard drive.

App Does Not Launch
If the installer finishes but the app does not open, check your Taskbar hidden icons. The application may be running in the background. If you receive a "Missing DLL" error, contact the community support forums to ensure your version of Windows 11 is fully updated through the Settings menu.

💻 System Compatibility

  • Active internet connection for the installation phase.
  • Standard user access rights to modify app settings.
